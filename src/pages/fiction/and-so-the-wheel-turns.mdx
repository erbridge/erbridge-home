And so the wheel turns.<br />
The bracken breaks beneath my feet,<br />
As these words roll over my tongue.

The wheel rolls over my tongue,<br />
As the bracken turns.<br />
And so these words break beneath my feet.

As the wheel breaks beneath my feet,<br />
And so the bracken rolls over my tongue,<br />
These words turn.

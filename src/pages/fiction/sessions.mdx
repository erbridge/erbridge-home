Thoughts and feelings,<br />
Overwhelming. passions and<br />
Truths. multiple and deafening.

To be under five needles. six voices. nine spirits.<br />
One journey. one story. one movement.<br />
Together.

Threading ink through skin.<br />
Open hearts,<br />
Touched. connected. joined. mended.

Time of inspiration.<br />
Of discovery.<br />
Time of friendship.
